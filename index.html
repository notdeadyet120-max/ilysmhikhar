A Letter For You

Dear Shikhar,

I don’t really know where to begin, because there are so many things I wish I could put into words.

From the day we started talking, something quietly changed in me. I started looking forward to every notification, every conversation, every little moment we shared.

Somehow, without even realizing it, you became a part of my thoughts.

I love the way you smile.
I love listening to you talk and the way you express yourself.

What makes you special isn’t just one thing — it’s everything about you.
Your kindness, your strength, your presence, your heart.

Even when life gets hard, you keep going. That strength is something I deeply admire.

You may not realize it, but you are truly rare and meaningful.

I’m not asking you to change anything or feel anything you don’t.

I just want you to know how deeply special you are to me.

Whether we talk for hours or just say goodnight, those moments matter more than I can explain.

You inspire me to become a better person.

I genuinely want you to be happy, peaceful, and successful in life.

If I ever get the chance to make you smile a little more than yesterday, I’ll consider myself lucky.

Thank you for simply being you.

— Siddhi ❤️
