A Letter For You

Dear Shikhar,

I don’t really know where to begin, because there are so many things I wish I could put into words.

From the day we started talking, something quietly changed in me. I started looking forward to every notification, every conversation, every little interaction we shared.

Somehow, without even realizing it, you became a part of my daily thoughts.

I love the way you smile.
I love listening to you talk and hearing your thoughts.
I love the warmth in your presence.

What makes you special to me isn't just one thing. It's everything about you—your kindness, your strength, your heart, and the way you keep moving forward no matter what life throws at you.

You may not always realize it, but there is something incredibly rare about you. Your presence feels comforting. Your words stay with me longer than you probably know.

I admire how strong you are. Even after everything, you keep going. That resilience in you is something truly beautiful.

I know you carry things silently sometimes. I know life can feel heavy. But even then, you continue to stand strong, and I hope you know how admirable that is.

I’m not asking you to forget your past. I’m not asking you to force any feelings.

I simply wanted you to know how deeply special you are to me.

Whether we're talking for hours or just saying goodnight, those little moments mean more to me than I can explain.

You inspire me to become a better person. You make me want to care more, understand more, and love more gently.

I genuinely want to see you happy. I want to see you peaceful, successful, and surrounded by everything you deserve.

If one day I get the chance to make you smile a little more than yesterday, I'd consider myself truly lucky.

Thank you for simply being you.

— Siddhi ❤️
